**The table `t` below represents four animals from the shelter:**

[cols='6',options="header"]
|===
| name        | sex       | age   | fixed   | species | pounds
| `"Toggle"`  | `"female"`| `12`  | `true`  | `"dog"` | `48`
| `"Fritz"`   | `"male"`  |  `4`  | `false` | `"dog"` | `92`
