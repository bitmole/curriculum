@vspace{2ex}

Draw a line from each arithmetic expression on the left to the corresponding Circle of Evaluation on the right.

@do{
  (define expr1 '(* (- 1 1) (+ 1 1)))
  (define expr2 '(- (- 1 1) 1))
  (define expr3 '(* (- 1 1) 1))
  (define expr4 '(- (+ 1 1) 1))
  (define expr5 '(* (+ 1 1) (- (+ 1 1) 1)))
}
